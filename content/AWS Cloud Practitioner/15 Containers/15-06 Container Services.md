## Primary Services

### Elastic Container Service (ECS)

* ![35](images/icons/ECS_Icon.png)
* No Cold Starts
* Self-Managed EC2

### AWS Fargate

* ![35](images/icons/Fargate_Icon.png)
* More Robust Than Lambda
* Scale to Zero Cost
* AWS-Managed EC2

### Elastic Kubernetes Services (EKS)

* ![35](images/icons/EKS_Icon.png)

* Avoid Vendor Lock-In
